Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a rapidly growing field that focuses on building systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence.

Machine Learning (ML), a subset of AI, allows systems to learn from data and improve their performance over time without being explicitly programmed. Deep Learning, a further subset, uses neural networks to model complex patterns in data.

AI is widely used in various industries such as healthcare, finance, transportation, and entertainment. Examples include chatbots, recommendation systems, self-driving cars, and fraud detection systems.

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is another important area of AI that enables machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language. Large Language Models (LLMs) like GPT are powerful tools used in NLP tasks such as text generation, summarization, and translation.

Despite its benefits, AI also raises ethical concerns such as data privacy, bias, and job displacement. Therefore, responsible AI development and usage are crucial for the future.
